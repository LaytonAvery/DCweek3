All About Alpacas (and Llamas)

Did you know that alpacas and llamas are cousins? While both of these animals may seem very similar, we will soon delve into their key characteristics, showcasing their many differences.

There are a few important facts about alpacas and llamas:

  • Alpacas are timid and kind while llamas tend to be protective guardians.

  • Llamas can weigh up to 450lbs while alpacas weigh between 100-200lbs.

  • Both animals are herbivores and can cost as much as $200,000 each to purchase.
